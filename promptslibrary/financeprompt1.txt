Comprehensive Financial Model for a Startup
Objective:   Create a dynamic financial model for a technology startup that projects revenue, expenses, cash flow, and profitability over a 5-year horizon.
Scope & Assumptions:
Revenue streams: subscription fees, one-time product sales, and advertising.
Growth assumptions: subscription base grows at 20% annually, product sales increase at 10% annually, advertising grows at 15% annually.
Cost structure: fixed costs (rent, salaries, utilities), variable costs (marketing, customer acquisition, server hosting).
Financing: initial seed funding of $500,000, potential Series A in Year 2.
Tax rate: 25%.
Inflation: 3% annually.
Outputs Required:
Income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement.
Break-even analysis.
Scenario analysis (best case, base case, worst case).
Key ratios: gross margin, net margin, return on equity, current ratio.
Charts: revenue growth, expense breakdown, cash runway.
Format:
Spreadsheet-style model with linked formulas.
Clear separation of inputs, calculations, and outputs.
Sensitivity analysis using adjustable variables.
